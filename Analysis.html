Welcome to Our Analytical Testing Services

At Alpha_Analytica, we specialize in delivering precise and reliable analytical testing solutions across various industries. Our comprehensive range of services includes chemical, physical, microbiological, and environmental testing, ensuring your materials, products, and processes meet the highest standards of quality and compliance. With state-of-the-art technology and a team of experienced professionals, we are committed to supporting your quality control, regulatory compliance, and research and development needs. Trust us to provide accurate insights and exceptional service for all your analytical testing requirements.

We Offer:

Heavy metals analysis using AAS

At Alpha_Analytica, we specialize in the precise detection and quantification of heavy metals using Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS). Our advanced AAS technology is ideal for a wide range of applications, ensuring that you receive accurate and dependable results for all your heavy metal analysis needs.

Elements metals analysis using XRF

At Alpha_Analytica, we specialize in providing comprehensive elemental analysis of metals using state-of-the-art X-ray fluorescence (XRF) technology. Our services are designed to meet the needs of a wide range of industries, ensuring you get accurate and timely results for your metal analysis requirements.

Organics analysis using HPLC/GCMS

At Alpha_Analytica, we offer specialized analysis of organic compounds using cutting-edge High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS) technologies. Our services are tailored to meet the diverse needs of industries that require precise identification and quantification of organic substances.

Compounds identification Using FT-IR

At Alpha_Analytica, we specialize in the precise identification of organic and inorganic compounds using Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FT-IR). Our advanced FT-IR technology allows us to provide accurate and reliable results for a wide range of applications across various industries.
